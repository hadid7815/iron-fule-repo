BMI Information

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using your height and weight. It helps determine whether your body weight falls within a healthy range.

BMI Range Category
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 Normal Weight
25 – 29.9 Overweight
30+ Obese

Health Tips

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in nutrients.
  • Exercise regularly for at least 30 minutes daily.
  • Stay hydrated and sleep 7–8 hours.
  • Avoid excessive sugar and processed foods.
  • Monitor your BMI periodically.
